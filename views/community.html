Why practice art?

Practicing art daily will increase your skills, strengthen your discipline, and can be therapeutic for your mental health.

Motivation and Encouragement

We are a small community, but focused on being an accountability group to be inspired by, share with, and get feedback from will keep you excited to keep sharing and build a daily practice streak!
